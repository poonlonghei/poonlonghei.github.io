About Me

My name is Chavez L. H. Poon, I am a freelance website designer and developer based in Hong Kong

I was born and raised in Hong Kong, where I earned my Diploma of Vocational Education in Information Technology in December 2023.

Over the course of my career, I have worked as a Social Media Manager and Website Administrator at an NGO Organization.

Aside from being a website designer and developer, I'm also a founder and owner of a online clothing brand.

I believe the process of creating some stuffs from nothing is deeply rewarding and seeing your work come together and materialize on a computer screen is a feeling like no other.

I am familiar with HTML, CSS, WordPress, WooCommerce and Shopify.

Aside from technological, I am a devoted Roman Catholic for more than 10 years, and I love playing musics (Pipe Organ, Piano and Drum sets), hiking and being active in outdoor. I alsohave a keen interest in history and personal development.
