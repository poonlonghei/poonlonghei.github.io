As you know I have been designing websites since 2020, exactly right before the COVID-19 pandemic, so today I want to share my personal experiences that how I start designing websites.

Back to December 2019, during my first year in vocational college, I learnt how to use very simple HTML codes to design a static website, then one day I found out that my former secondary school, some of the teachers had their own personal homepage, hosted in the school's web server. So I started to think that I should create my very own personal homepage, while I learnt HTML from my vocational college, I started to find where should I host my website, after I compared the price of the domain and the hosting, I think I should use a free hosting because I don't want to use my money for the hosting and domain.

Luckily I found a free web hosting called freewha.com, so I registered a hosting service with freewha.com include with the free domain name, I still remembered the domain name is chavezpoon.freewha.com, but sadly I didn't upload it to Web Archive! Since then, I tried a lot of free web hosting provider such as Neocities and Space by W3Schools, then one day, I decided to pay a little bit to register a specific domain name for my website, which is chavezpoon.com.

After I registered my own domain name, I also added the hosting service by cPanel, which I can upload my HTML web files into the server, after a couple of years, I decided to make a change for my website, I installed WordPress to my website's server, so I can easily working on publishing my blog articles.

So why did I become a website designer and developer? Because some of my friends know that I'm a expert on designing websites, so they ask me to help them to build a website free of charges, for me, this is like a startup progress, to gain more experiences from your friend.

That's all for today, next time I'll talk about how to startup your career as a freelance website designer and developer!
